Green Detox Juice Recipe

  • HOME

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 green apples
  • 1 lemon
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • 1 cup of water (optional)

Directions:

  1. Wash all the fruits and vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Peel the cucumber and cut it into chunks.
  3. Remove the seeds from the apples and cut them into quarters.
  4. Peel the lemon and ginger.
  5. Place all the ingredients into a blender.
  6. Add water if desired for a thinner consistency.
  7. Blend until smooth.
  8. Strain the juice to remove any pulp.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy!
