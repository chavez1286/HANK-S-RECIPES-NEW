Peach and Arugula Salad Recipe

  • HOME

Ingredients:

  • Peaches
  • Arugula
  • Almonds
  • Feta Cheese
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Honey
  • Sea Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Directions:

  1. Wash and slice the peaches.
  2. In a large bowl, combine arugula, sliced peaches, almonds, and crumbled feta cheese.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together apple cider vinegar, honey, sea salt, black pepper, and olive oil to make the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat.
  5. Serve immediately.
