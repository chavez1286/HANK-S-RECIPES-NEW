Caesar Salad Recipe

  • HOME

Ingredients:

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Caesar Dressing
  • Black Pepper

Directions:

  1. Wash and dry the Romaine lettuce, then tear into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Add croutons and grated Parmesan cheese to the lettuce.
  3. Pour Caesar dressing over the salad.
  4. Toss the salad until evenly coated with dressing.
  5. Season with freshly ground black pepper to taste.
